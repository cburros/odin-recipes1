Chicken Quesadilla

Description

This is a great Chicken Quesadilla recipe that my ex wife came up with. I have not been able to find this version of the recipe in any restaurant.
I have considered starting a food truck and featuring this particular recipe on the food truck. I think it would be a big hit!

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts - Shredded
  • 1 Jar of Picante
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Large tortilla shells

Steps

  1. Mix the Picante sauce with the Cream Cheese
  2. Mix the sauce mixture with the shredded chicken
  3. Layer cheddar cheese on one half of a tortilla shell
  4. Place chicken and sauce mixture on top of cheese
  5. Fold tortilla over the chicken mixture
  6. Cook in a skillet on medium heat for 8 minutes, flipping halfway through.
