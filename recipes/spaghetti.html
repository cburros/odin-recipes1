Spaghetti

Description

I like to make Spaghetti, but I make it in a way that seems to make people mad. First of all, I use thinner, angel hair noodles. But the real travesty is that I break the spaghetti noodles before I put them in the pot. I want them all to cook at the same time. I also don't want to spend all my time twirling noodles on my fork.

Ingredients

  • 1 box Barilla Angel Hair Pasta
  • 1 Jar Prego Traditional Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with 4 quarts of hot water
  2. Add salt
  3. Brown ground beef
  4. Place water over medium heat
  5. Pour Spaghetti sauce in a small sauce pan and warm it over low heat
  6. Once water has begun to boil, break noodles in half and place in the pot of water
  7. Add ground beef to the spaghetti sauce
  8. After 7 minutes, drain water through a strainer
  9. Add sauce mixture to spaghetti
